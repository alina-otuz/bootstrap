FAQ

What is the origin of the Olympic Games?

The ancient Olympic Games began in Olympia, Greece, in 776 BC to honor Zeus.

When were the modern Olympics revived?

They were revived in 1896 in Athens, Greece, thanks to Pierre de Coubertin.

Which country hosted the most Olympic Games?

The United States has hosted the Olympics 8 times.
