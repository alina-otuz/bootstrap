About Olympic Games

The Olympic Games are the most prestigious sporting event in the world, bringing together athletes from every continent.

History

The ancient Olympic Games began in 776 BC in Olympia, Greece. The modern Olympics were revived in 1896 in Athens by Pierre de Coubertin.

FAQ

Where are the next Olympic Games?

In 2026, the Winter Olympics will be held in Milano-Cortina, Italy.

Who founded the modern Olympics?

Pierre de Coubertin founded the modern Olympic Games in 1896.

How often are the Olympics held?

Every four years, alternating between Summer and Winter Games.
